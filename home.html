About Us

College Buzz is a centralized platform that simplifies college communication, providing instant notifications, event registrations, and seamless management for colleges and students. It ensures that students never miss out on important updates while allowing colleges to efficiently manage events.

Why College Buzz?

  • Information Overload: Important updates get lost in WhatsApp groups.
  • Missed Deadlines: Students often forget event registrations.
  • Lack of Organization: Searching for old messages is inefficient.
  • Unprofessional Communication: Colleges need a structured way to notify students.

Solution: College Buzz provides a professional, real-time notification system!
